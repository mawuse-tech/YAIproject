Meet The Founder

Oyibo Electric Power and Robotics (OEPR) was founded by Mr. Francis Quansah, a passionate innovator dedicated to creating sustainable and reliable energy solutions. Driven by the need for consistent power in remote and underserved communities, He developed OEPR to operate independently of traditional renewable sources like biomass, wind, and solar.

Using advanced technologies like thermoelectric generators, piezoelectric generators, and hydrogen fuel cells, Mr. Francis Quansah designed a system that efficiently generates and stores energy. Their work with OEPR aims to promote sustainability, reduce environmental pollution, and enhance energy independence and resilience.
