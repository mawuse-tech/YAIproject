Meet The Founder

Mr. Francis Quansah is an entrepreneur from Gomoa West in the Central Region, who is tackling the challenge of high energy consumption and reliance on traditional power sources for refrigeration and building power. His solution involves developing an energy-efficient refrigerator and synchronized motor power plant, offering a sustainable alternative that reduces dependence on conventional energy

His expectation is to receive support and resources to refine and expand his innovation, connect with experts in sustainable energy, and gain exposure and recognition for his work. He aims to make a significant impact by providing accessible, eco-friendly refrigeration and power solutions.

About the Product

Discover the vision and innovation behind this energy-generating machine.

An electrical power system

Built with Skill, Powered by Purpose

The innovation, Oyibo Electric Power System, is about creating a self-sustaining energy system that operates independently of traditional renewable energy sources such as biomass, wind, or solar. It leverages advanced technologies like thermoelectric generators, piezoelectric generators, and hydrogen fuel cells to generate and store energy efficiently. The system aims to provide a consistent and reliable power supply, particularly for remote and underserved communities, by utilizing alternative methods of energy generation. This solution promotes sustainability, reduces environmental pollution, and enhances energy independence and resilience
